What Is an Email Bot? 
An email bot is an automated marketing tool that can respond quickly to incoming email leads. 
Using Artificial Intelligence and contextual analysis, an email bot delivers customized email responses, 
allowing you to automatically respond with the right message and engage potential leads.
How do I make a Gmail bot?
Add bots in direct messages
1.Open Gmail .
2.In the Chat section, select a direct message.
3.At the top, click Menu. next to the person, or list of people in the chat.
4.Click Add bots.
5.Enter the name of the bot, or select from the suggestions.
6.Click Add.
